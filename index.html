A2SV is an academy that empowers university students to create digital solutions to Africa's most pressing problems. We provide free, comprehensive in-person and remote education programs designed to prepare you for a successful career in tech.
Here are some of the benefits of joining A2SV:
- Gain strong understanding of Data Structures and Algorithms.
- Develop problem-solving and critical thinking abilities.
- Gain hands-on experience working on real-world projects.
- Connect with a network of tech professionals and potential mentors

1. In-person Education

For students currently enrolled at our partner universities in Ethiopia (Addis Ababa University, Addis Ababa Science and Technology University, and Adama Science and Technology University) and Ghana (University of Legon), we offer in-person education programs.
As part of our collaboration with partner universities, we offer dormitory accommodation and offer the training program by our experienced trainers who come directly to your campus. The first year of in-person A2SV education consists of learning phases, intensive camps, and a culminating project phase.

2. Remote Education

For those not enrolled at our partner universities, recent graduates, or high school students, we offer a comprehensive remote education program. This flexible program allows you to learn from anywhere in the world, ensuring that everyone has access to quality tech education.
For our remote education program, students receive an unlimited internet package, ensuring seamless access to online resources. Our experienced trainers will deliver the year-long training program, mirroring the structure of our in-person program.

3. Community Education Program: Gateway to A2SV's Programs

Our Community Education Program serves as the gateway to both in-person and remote education opportunities. This introductory program provides a foundation of essential tech skills and knowledge, preparing you for success in our advanced programs.
Upon completing your self-paced learning path and participating in two A2SV Community contests, you'll gain access to our Community Education Program, where our experienced community coordinators will equip you with essential data structures like stacks, queues, and linked lists, along with fundamental algorithms like sorting algorithms, two-pointers, sliding window, and recursion. To familiarize you with the A2SV interview process, we'll provide live mock interviews, showcasing real A2SV interview scenarios. Our Community Education Program is conducted remotely, and we expect an attendance commitment of over 85%.

In-person Education:

To participate in our in-person education programs, you must be currently enrolled as a student at one of our partner universities: Addis Ababa University, Addis Ababa Science and Technology University, Adama University, or the University of Legon.

Remote Education:

Our remote education programs are open to graduates, non-graduates, and high school students across the African continent. However, you cannot be currently enrolled as a student at one of our partner universities.

Community Education Program:

The Community Education Program welcomes anyone with a passion for problem-solving and programming. No prior experience or education in technology is required to participate in this program
